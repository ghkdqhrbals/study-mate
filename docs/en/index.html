StudyMate

The better you understand, the better AI works.

Using AI every day taught me one thing: the more I understand a subject, the sharper my questions become and the clearer the output gets. I built StudyMate to keep that habit alive and share it.

v1.0.8 MIT License Free software
Download DMG GitHub

The app is free. You only need your own OpenAI API key.

What it helps with

  • Get questions by topic and difficulty.
  • Answer and receive a score, feedback, and explanation.
  • Review records and statistics to see what you know.

A simple principle

The goal is to keep building your own knowledge, so AI stays a tool for clearer thinking instead of a replacement for it.
